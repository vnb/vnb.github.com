Pricing optimization with A/B test

Broadly speaking, A/B testing is a method to compare betweem two datasets that are randomly assigned to a certain treatment(treatment group) or no treament(control group). It has applications in web design, marketing and even in pricing optimization since the results are easy to obtain. This post mainly focuses on A/B testing for pricing optimization with the end goal to increase teh revenue.
